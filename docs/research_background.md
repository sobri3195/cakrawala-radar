# Research Background

Indonesia is an archipelagic country with wide airspace, strategic routes, tropical weather, and interoperability challenges. Cakrawala Radar provides a simplified environment for exploring:

- multi-sensor uncertainty,
- route and coverage analysis,
- synthetic ADS-B/radar comparison,
- track continuity,
- rule-based anomaly detection,
- explainable summaries for safety-oriented research.

The implementation is intentionally approximate. It is designed for teaching, prototyping, and reproducible experiments, not operational surveillance.
